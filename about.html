Hi! I'm Olga

I'm a digital artist. I specialize in pixel art, digital illustration and animation. My work is inspired by nature, storytelling, and a deep appreciation for vibrant colors and detail. I have a passion for creating engaging art that tells a story, whether it's a single character or a whole world.

Resume

I have a diverse range of experience in both the digital art and design industries, including freelance work and personal projects. I have worked as a freelance artist on platforms like Upwork and Fiverr, creating pixel art, illustrations, and animations.

Selected Clients

Freelance Projects on Upwork and Fiverr, various collaborations and commissions

My Work Process

Each project begins with understanding the client's vision and needs. I collaborate closely with clients, ensuring their ideas are reflected in the final product. Whether it's creating pixel art or designing a website, I always aim for clean, professional, and visually appealing results. I use tools like Adobe Photoshop, Blender and other digital softwares to bring my concepts to life.

My Interests

When I'm not working on creative projects, I enjoy painting, going for walks in nature, and exploring new art styles. I'm a big fan of strategy games and love the challenge they provide. Additionally, I continue learning languages and discovering new creative techniques. I'm also vegan, and I enjoy experimenting with plant-based recipes in my free time.
